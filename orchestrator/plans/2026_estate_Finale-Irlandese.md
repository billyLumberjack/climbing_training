## 3. Rationale

- **W1 (bridge):** No fitness can be built in one week, so it serves the 25–26 Jul boulder weekend: one midweek indoor skill/repertoire session, maintenance hangboard Monday (today), light Friday physical. Fingers and skin fresh for two outdoor boulder days — which themselves are repertoire + blocked-practice gold (the pattern that produced the 7a/7a+ sends).
- **Holiday deload (2 wks):** After three consecutive mesos, a real deload is a feature, not a loss. BW travel templates (proven in Meso 3 W5) preserve finger and pull-chain qualities at 1–2 touches/week; opportunistic climbing on unfamiliar terrain is exactly the random practice the on-sight goal needs. RPE targets are ceilings — it's a holiday.
- **W2 (re-entry):** First week back ramps deliberately (counts at the low end, RPE capped) — re-entry weeks after full breaks are where fingers get hurt. By the weekend, normal loading.
- **W3–W4 (build → peak):** The 3-2-1 logic. W3 leads with movement repertoire and boulder power (varied-style problems, weakness-first while fresh, random practice), with the anaerobic-endurance thread starting midweek. W4 is the volume peak and tilts toward pump management: intervals at 1:1 work:rest, the 120–180 s TUT progression carried over from Meso 3, and fatigue-state skill recall — the physiological signature of on-sight leading. Hangboard goes to 2×/week and returns to a **light progressive thread** (rebuilding toward Meso 2 working loads after two maintenance mesos) — fingers are fresh and the autumn meso will want them strong, but no PR chasing here.
- **W5 (integration + mini-taper):** Reduced volume, held intensity. Climbing shifts to on-sight simulation (never-tried routes/problems ground-up, pre-climb reading ritual, mid-route reading at rests). The meso hands off a rested, movement-rich athlete to the autumn on-sight meso rather than peaking here — the peak belongs to autumn.
- **RPE discipline (recurring Meso 3 lesson):** prescribed RPE is a target, not a floor. Overshooting mileage/skill work inhibits exactly the acquisition this meso is for.
